About Me

The Girl from Mexico City
Who Chose the Ocean

Born and raised in the vibrant heart of Mexico City, Paola discovered early on that her greatest gift was making people feel something — laughter, wonder, connection. That gift led her to the world of cruise ship entertainment, where she has built a career as a live entertainer, host, and audience engagement specialist.

Whether she's hosting bingo, commanding a stage in the main theater, or creating content for her growing TikTok community as @paodecrucero, Paola brings the same unstoppable energy: warm, charismatic, and completely real.

My Why

Philosophy & Heart

"I believe every person who steps onto a cruise ship deserves to feel something extraordinary — and it's my job to make sure they do."

Entertainment isn't just a job for me — it's a calling. Growing up in Mexico City, I learned that the most powerful thing you can give someone is a moment of pure joy. That belief is what drives me every single day on stage, on deck, and in front of the camera.

My dream of becoming a Cruise Director isn't just about a title. It's about leading a team that creates magic for thousands of guests from every corner of the world. It's about building programs that are inclusive, energetic, and deeply human.

On TikTok, I share the real, unfiltered life at sea — the early mornings, the stage lights, the friendships formed across oceans. I'm passionate about showing the world what this career truly looks like, and inspiring others — especially young women from Latin America — to chase their most ambitious dreams.

The ocean has taught me that there are no limits — only horizons. And I intend to reach every single one. 🌊
